Uses of Computing in the Medical Field

The author of this page is Eila

There are many uses for computers in the medical field



3D visualisation of the human body

Doctors can view the body and learn in a "hands on" way.

This can help reduce the time it takes to diagnose patients and

can give the doctor more information, faster.



Virtual Operations

Top doctors from around the world can virtually perform surgery

This means that a patient who cannot get transportation for a key

surgery can still recieve it wherever they are
